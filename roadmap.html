Ongoing Development

This product will continue to be developed as a volunteer based project with GIS Corps. Future development will focus on:

  • streamlining and strengthening data contribution from community members,
  • further development of the atlas interface for greater functionality,
  • enabling offline access for use in areas with limited mobile data service, and
  • developing printed maps.
